Caregiver Action Network (CAN) understands these challenges and has developed resources aimed at providing financial help for caregivers. Through their#CaregiversConnectinitiative, CAN is helping family caregivers better manage finances while continuing to provide the best care possible.Financial Help for Caregivers: The #CaregiversConnect InitiativeThe CaregiversConnect initiative is designed to provide financial help for caregivers through access to reliable tools and expert services. These resources support caregivers in managing household bills, budgeting, and organizing legal documents, making it easier for them to navigate their loved ones financial responsibilities. By easing the financial burden on caregivers, the initiative allows them to focus more on providing essential care.Partnering with SilverBills: Financial Help for CaregiversCaregiver Action Network has partnered with SilverBills, a trusted financial concierge service. SilverBills offers essential financial help for caregivers by managing household bills, ensuring payments are made on time, and offering budgeting assistance and legal document storage. Since 2014, SilverBills has helped alleviate the financial pressures many caregivers face, ensuring that their loved ones finances are taken care of without the added stress of daily management.NIH Research Study: Financial Help for Dementia and Alzheimers CaregiversIn partnership with SilverBills, the National Institutes of Health (NIH) is conducting aNIH-funded research studyaimed at providing financial help for caregivers of Dementia and Alzheimers patients. Caregivers of individuals with these conditions often face heightened financial and emotional stress due to the ongoing care requirements. The study aims to determine whether SilverBills services, including bill management, budgeting, and secure document storage, can alleviate some of the stress experienced by these caregivers. The research also examines how SilverBills services improve the quality of life for caregivers, allowing them to focus more on their loved ones well-being.Exclusive Incentives for Study ParticipantsAs part of the NIH study, qualified participants will receive exciting incentives, including a$100 Amazon Gift Cardandone year of free accessto SilverBills financial management services. These incentives are designed to encourage caregivers to participate in the study and benefit from the tools provided. Participants will have the opportunity to experience first-hand how SilverBills services can simplify their financial responsibilities while giving them more time to focus on caregiving. Learn more about the study here:https://www.silverbills.com/nih-study.Please note that participants may be asked to provide personal or financial documentation as part of the study enrollment process.
